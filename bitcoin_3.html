Bitcoin's Eternal Flame

In the digital forge of 2009, Satoshi ignited Bitcoin's eternal flame against fiat darkness. Proof-of-work hammered scarcity into code: 21 million unyielding. From cypherpunk whispers to global thunder, it shattered banks' monopolies.

Halvings like cosmic clocks ticked value moons: $0 to $100k+. Forks tested, bans crushed—Bitcoin antifragile phoenix. Lightning lit peer-to-peer fire; ETFs poured nation-state gold.

Now, nations stack sats, corps custody keys. HODLers chant: "Be your bank!" Hyperbitcoinization dawns—freedom's blockchain unbreakable.
